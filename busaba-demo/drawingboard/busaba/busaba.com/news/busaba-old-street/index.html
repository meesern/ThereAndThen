Busaba Old Street

Teach a man to fish and you will feed him for a lifetime.

Busaba is proud to be open in East London’s thriving Shoreditch region. With so much natural and raw creativity, multicultural and effortless charm, the area has long been attractive, not only from a business perspective but also in keeping with the original mantra of the company.

When Busaba first opened ten years ago on Wardour Street, it wanted to offer savvy Soho (another of London’s creative energy hubs) sound, fair priced, well executed, flavour-packed Thai food. Openings in Store Street, Bird Street and Panton Street, all within the West End ensued, albeit slowly aiming each time to deliver beyond expectations.

Opening on Old Street is likened to opening in Soho ten years ago. Again we have a unique audience, a mixed bag of savvy consumers centered around a district of dynamic imaginative energy. In keeping with the Busaba ethos of Sanuk and by way of an introduction, Busaba aims to positively contribute and support the local community on an ongoing basis. On this occasion whilst works underwent inside to develop the concept, rather than pitching up archetypal branded hoarding, Busaba dedicated this space to a sequence of local artists; graphic designers, illustrators and photographers so they could have a platform to exhibit their work whilst delivering an amusing space to Hoxton residents.

It took some time to find the ideal Old Street site, the spatial configuration, logistical and operational elements all had to be aligned. The Busaba offering remains the same; sound, fair priced, well executed, flavour-packed Thai food. We strive to do more, to continue the development of our relations whilst working on inventive ways to engage with the local community.*

Sorry it took a while to find our East London home but we were still at sea - learning to fish.

*To contact us for future creative projects please email sanuk@busaba.com
